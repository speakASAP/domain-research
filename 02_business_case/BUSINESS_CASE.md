# Business Case

New projects repeatedly need domain names. One-time manual WHOIS checks are easy to forget and do not produce reliable follow-up when a registered name expires.

Domain Research reduces this waste by making domain discovery, availability evidence, and expiry follow-up a managed workflow.

Success means users can:

- describe an idea;
- receive candidate domains;
- see availability and expiry evidence;
- watch registered domains;
- receive a notification only after a scheduled recheck shows the domain is available.
