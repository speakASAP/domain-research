# GOAL-IMPACT-TASK-004 Expiring Domain Drop Tracking

## Vision

Domain Research must warn users before a watched registered domain is likely to become generally available, while keeping every availability claim evidence-backed.

## Goal Impact

Supports Goal 1 and Goal 4 by extending watch scheduling from simple expiry rechecks into lifecycle-aware drop tracking and user notifications.

## User Impact

Users can be warned about one-week, one-day, one-hour, and actual-availability moments so they can prepare to buy a domain manually through a registrar.

## Risk Impact

The service must not guarantee exact drop time for every TLD. The drop timestamp is an estimate unless RDAP/registry status provides stronger evidence.
