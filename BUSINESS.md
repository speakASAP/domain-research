# Business Intent

## Vision

Make domain discovery and lifecycle monitoring a repeatable service inside Alfares: users can describe what they are building, receive strong domain-name options, understand whether those names are available or registered, and get notified when watched registered domains become available.

## Users

- Alfares product owners launching new applications.
- Business builders who need brandable domain names.
- Internal agents that need domain availability evidence before naming a service or campaign.

## Outcomes

- Better domain names are proposed from business context.
- Availability checks are evidence-backed and repeatable.
- Expiry dates become scheduled checks instead of one-time notes.
- Users receive notifications only after a domain is rechecked and appears available.

## Non-Goals

- The MVP does not automatically buy domains.
- The MVP does not store raw WHOIS/RDAP payloads unless a later compliance task approves it.
- The MVP does not implement a custom notification delivery stack.
- The MVP does not replace docs-rag-microservice.
